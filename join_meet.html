Why Weekends Matter

While weekdays cover concepts, our Saturday & Sunday sessions are non-negotiable for success.

  • Deep Dives: We go beyond theory into complex coding problems.
  • Project Review: Real-time feedback on your agricultural AI models.
  • Peer Coding: Collaborative debugging sessions that you miss during the week.

Preparation Advice

To get the most out of every session, we advise you to:

  • Join 5 minutes early to test your audio/video.
  • Review your notes from the previous session.
  • Have your code editor (VS Code/Colab) open and ready.
  • Prepare at least one question regarding the week's topic.
